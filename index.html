Worst Holiday. What’s the worst holiday of the year? New Year’s? Halloween? Christmas? No, of course not. It’s Valentine’s Day, My worst enemy, The thing I hate most in the world, Because it’s all about love. Love. A huge joke, the rest of The world tucked in its strong grasp, Just a joke. I used to have love in my heart, Taken away from me in an instant, Though. In the blink of an eye, Everything stopped. The sound of my heartbeat Had slowed to a halt, And in a split second, It started back up again. Fast Paced. So yes, I hate Valentine’s Day. Good for other people, Not me though. Love. The dreaded word. Something I don’t have, Something I will never have. The newness of New Year’s, The horror of Halloween, The jolly ways of Christmas. All of it better than love.
